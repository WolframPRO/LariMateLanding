Your Top Questions, Answered

Which exchange rate does LariMate use?

LariMate exclusively uses the official daily exchange rates published by the National Bank of Georgia (NBG), which is the legal requirement for tax reporting.

Is my financial data secure?

Absolutely. Your data is stored securely in your private iCloud account and is never shared with us or any third parties. It syncs seamlessly and privately between your Apple devices.

Do I still need an accountant?

For most Individual Entrepreneurs with the 1% small business status and straightforward income, LariMate provides all the tools you need to manage your own tax declarations. If you have a more complex situation, we always recommend consulting a professional.
